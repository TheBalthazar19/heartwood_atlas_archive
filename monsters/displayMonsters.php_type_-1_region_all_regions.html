Canis

Bone Mines

Monster Notes



A much larger, supernatural canine skeleton. At half health, Canis will turn red and enter a 'frenzy' mode and start rapidly hurling large bones that can temporarily trap players if they hit their mark.

 

Bosses no longer spawn routinely, but you can summon Canis by placing a Blood Offering on the shrine in Feasting Den (see map).

Players can spawn multiple bosses at a time, as long as they have the offerings. The player who makes the offering is the only one eligible to gain aggro once spawned.

Players can be grouped to fight Canis, but if the summoning player leaves the room or dies during combat it will regain full health. If the summoning player logs off, Canis will disappear.

Monster Notes



A towering and terrifying skeletal force residing in the 'Well of Sorrow' at the center of Miner's Hollow.

The Overseer has several abilities:
  • Occasionally jumps high in the air before stomping, dropping boulders (which can be dodged) when he lands.

  • Causes spontaneous eruptions of spikes from the ground. Red circles warn players just before they emerge and damage over time is inflicted in those areas while the spikes are active.

  • At half health, Overseer begins hurling large bones which burst on impact, releasing smaller bones with potential to temporarily trap players.

  • After 5 minutes of combat Overseer will get aggravated, turning red and dealing greater damage.
 

Bosses no longer spawn routinely, but you can summon Overseer by placing a Soul Offering on the shrine in Feasting Den (see map).

Players can spawn multiple bosses at a time, as long as they have the offerings. The player who makes the offering is the only one eligible to gain aggro once spawned.

Players can be grouped to fight Overseer, but if the summoning player leaves the room or dies during combat it will regain full health. If the summoning player logs off, Overseer will disappear.
